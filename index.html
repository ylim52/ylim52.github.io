Minimizing Fuel Consumption under the Traffic Regulation

- Designed and implemented using Julia and Gurobi to minimize the fuel consumption for delivery companies under the Madison traffic regulation.



- Inspired by “Why UPS drivers never turn left” and most of the delivery companies struggle to reduce the fuel consumption of the delivery vehicle.



- Created mathematical models such as Mixed Integer Linear Programming (MILP) and Mixed Integer Programming (MIP) to analyze the real-world data in Madison, WI.

Introduction to Model-Free Reinforcement Learning and Applications

- Provided background knowledge of Reinforcement Learning to introduce Model-Free Reinforcement Learning (MFRL).



- Expanded the concept of MFRL by using Q-learning and State-Action-Reward-State-Action (SARSA) and applied it to game playing and robotics.



- Created own mathematical model problems based on our concepts of MFRL and suggested their solutions.
